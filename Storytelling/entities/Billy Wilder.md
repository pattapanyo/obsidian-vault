# Billy Wilder

## Overview
Austrian-born American filmmaker (1906–2002). One of the most versatile and celebrated screenwriters and directors of Hollywood's Golden Age. His career spanned more than fifty years and sixty films, ranging from film noir to screwball comedy. The first person in history to win Academy Awards as producer, director, and writer for the same film — *The Apartment* (1960).

## Key Films
- *Double Indemnity* (1944) — film noir, Oscar for Best Screenplay
- *Sunset Boulevard* (1950) — Oscar for Best Screenplay
- *Some Like It Hot* (1959)
- *The Apartment* (1960) — Best Picture, Director, Screenplay
