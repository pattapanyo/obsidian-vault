# Cameron Crowe

## Overview
American writer-director known for *Fast Times at Ridgemont High*, *Jerry Maguire*, and *Almost Famous*. Approached Billy Wilder in 1996 about a small role in *Jerry Maguire* — Wilder declined but the two formed a friendship. Their conversations over several years became the book *Conversations with Wilder* (1999), which contains Wilder's 10 rules of screenwriting.
