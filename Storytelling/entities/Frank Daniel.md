# Frank Daniel

## Overview
Czech-American filmmaker and screenwriting teacher. Headed the film programs at FAMU (Prague), Columbia University, and USC. Developed the eight-sequence approach to screenplay structure, which became one of the most widely taught structural frameworks in American film schools.

## Key Contributions
- Originated [[concepts/eight-sequence-structure]]
- Influenced a generation of screenwriting educators and practitioners through his teaching at major film schools
