Storytelling/
├── AI.md ← schema + instructions for the AI

├── entities/ ← writers, directors, films, books

## Workflows
### Ingest
When given a source:
1. Write a sources/ page
2. Update/create relevant concepts/ & entities/
3. Provide segmented instructions for index.md and log.md

### Query
1. Answer with citations to wiki pages.
2. Offer to file substantial answers as synthesis/ pages.

### Lint
1. Flag orphans, contradictions, and stale claims.
2. Suggest new pages for missing links.
